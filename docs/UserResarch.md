- Observation 1
    - Observation Statement: Most potential customers want the colors of SmartFuel to be green and blue.
    - Supporting Data: 23.6% of respondants' color votes included green and 23.6% of respondants' color votes included blue.
    - Team Action: We have decided to make the main colors of the Smart Fuel app green and blue to cater towards our customers. 

- Observation 2
    - Observation Statement: Most potential customers do not currently use an app or website to check gas prices.
    - Supporting Data: 94% of users said voted ‘No’ to ‘Do you currently use an app or website to check gas prices?’.
    - Team Action (if any): We decided to push the idea that SmartFuel will help users find better deals on gas.
